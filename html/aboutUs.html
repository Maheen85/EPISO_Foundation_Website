About Us

Our Vision

Episo Foundation (LOI Submitted to George W. Hill Prison Facility): Established in 2017, the Episo Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit whose goal is to educate, equip and fortify women (who will be identified as “Fellows” throughout the LOI) from: low-income backgrounds, currently incarcerated, ex-offenders and their children with the tools to independently build generational wealth. The Episo Foundation will tackle systemic educational inequality and employment (wage) inequity by providing technical training and employment opportunities within the industries of technology and real estate. The Episo Foundation has developed what is known as the Carbonado Initiative which will address three major areas of concern such as: reducing the recidivism rates, providing job readiness skills and assisting with obtaining housing for our “Fellows” who are or have been incarcerated
